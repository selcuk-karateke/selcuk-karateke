# Instagram Business API - Usage Descriptions für App Review

## 1. instagram_business_basic

**Usage Description:**
My content management platform needs to access Instagram business account information to enable content creation, management, and real-time analytics across multiple Instagram accounts. This permission allows my app to retrieve basic account details such as Instagram username, profile picture, follower count, and account verification status. This information is essential for managing multiple client accounts, verifying account access, providing account-specific content creation features, and displaying real-time account statistics for content planning and performance tracking. Without this permission, my platform cannot identify or manage the Instagram accounts that users want to create content for, nor can it provide real-time account insights.

**How it helps users:**
- Enables account verification and access management
- Provides account-specific content creation features
- Shows real-time account statistics for content planning
- Allows management of multiple client accounts
- Displays live follower count and engagement metrics
- Provides account verification status for credibility

**Why my app needs it:**
- To verify Instagram account access
- To provide account-specific content creation
- To manage multiple client accounts
- To display real-time account information for content planning
- To show live account statistics and metrics
- To provide account verification status

**How my app uses the data:**
- Verifies account access and permissions
- Displays real-time account information for content creation
- Manages multiple Instagram accounts
- Provides account-specific features
- Shows live follower count and engagement metrics
- Updates account statistics in real-time

**Why less useful without it:**
- Cannot verify Instagram account access
- No account-specific content creation
- Cannot manage multiple accounts
- No real-time account statistics
- Limited content management capabilities
- Missing live engagement metrics

## 2. instagram_business_content

**Usage Description:**
My content management platform needs to create, publish, manage, and display Instagram posts and reels across multiple business accounts. This permission allows my app to upload media, create captions, schedule posts, publish content, manage existing posts and stories, and retrieve real-time content data for display and analytics. This functionality is essential for providing comprehensive content management services to clients who want to maintain active Instagram presence, while also showcasing content performance and engagement metrics in real-time. Without this permission, my platform cannot create or publish content, nor can it display real-time content analytics, making it impossible to provide complete content management services.

**How it helps users:**
- Creates and publishes Instagram posts and reels
- Schedules content for optimal posting times
- Manages content across multiple accounts
- Provides content creation and editing tools
- Displays real-time content performance and engagement
- Shows live post analytics and metrics
- Provides content portfolio and showcase features

**Why my app needs it:**
- To create and publish Instagram content
- To schedule posts and reels
- To manage content across multiple accounts
- To provide content management services
- To display real-time content analytics
- To showcase content performance and engagement
- To provide content portfolio features

**How my app uses the data:**
- Uploads media files to Instagram
- Creates captions and hashtags
- Schedules posts for optimal times
- Manages and edits existing content
- Displays real-time post engagement metrics
- Shows live content performance analytics
- Updates content statistics in real-time

**Why less useful without it:**
- Cannot create or publish content
- No content scheduling capabilities
- Cannot provide content management services
- No real-time content analytics
- Limited to content viewing only
- Missing live engagement metrics

## 3. instagram_business_insights

**Usage Description:**
My content management platform needs to access Instagram business insights and analytics to provide data-driven content strategy, real-time performance reporting, and comprehensive analytics dashboard for multiple client accounts. This permission allows my app to retrieve engagement metrics, reach statistics, audience demographics, and performance data from Instagram business accounts. This data is essential for optimizing content strategy, measuring campaign effectiveness, providing comprehensive reporting to clients, and displaying real-time analytics dashboards with live performance metrics. Without this permission, my platform cannot provide analytics-based content recommendations, performance reports, or real-time insights dashboards.
